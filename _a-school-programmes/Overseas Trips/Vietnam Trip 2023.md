<p>The 2023 Youth Expedition Project team has returned from an enriching
journey to Vietnam, filled with meaningful experiences and lasting memories.
During the trip, the team completed a community garden project, constructing
flower beds and planting vegetable seeds, set to be harvested in just five
days. A highlight of the journey was meeting Mr. Harry Pham Van Anh, the
founder of ECO Vietnam Group, and collaborating with local communities.
The team organized an English games carnival at Phong Thanh Secondary School,
spreading joy and learning. Additionally, they had the chance to connect
with local businesses such as Grandma Lu, a popular banh mi cafe, and Goldeneye
Technologies, an innovative tech company. The team also embraced the warmth
of the Cau Ke community, teaching English and coding using Quarky, while
witnessing the students' enthusiasm for learning. This trip was a valuable
opportunity to serve, connect, and make a positive impact on the community,
reinforcing the spirit of Outram and our commitment to nurturing compassionate
and engaged global citizens.</p>
